## LinkedIn — cluster: the three NRC pathways

Quick version of a question every advanced reactor team asks at some point: Part 50, Part 52, or Part 53.

Short answer: Part 50 if you want flexibility and don't mind the two-step CP/OL process, Part 52 if you want the single Combined Licence and a Design Certification, Part 53 if your design philosophy is risk-informed and technology-inclusive.

Longer answer is here, with timelines and 2026 docket examples:
